Welcome to the world of warships

The world of warships is a diverse and facinating one. The designs and styles of the various ship types are truly amazing, the same type of ship looks vastly different from nation to nation. Beyond that ships are have a personality all to their own. They have a singular name, that only one ship at a time has, making them unique, and more personal thing.

The Destroyer.

The destroyer is a type of ship that was originally designed to be a ship armed to destroy torpedo boats. Later it mainly became escort ship, AA support, transport, anti-submarine warfare, and nowadays, a mainstay of any fleet.

The Cruiser.

The cruiser is the workhorse of the fleet, being capable of medium AA escort, shore bombardment, escort, flagship duties, and depending on the type, a heavy or light anti ship platform.

The Battleship.

The battleship was the center of the fleet, mounting massive guns, thick armor, and numerous smaller secondary guns. Later in their life they were heavy AA escorts, shore bombardment ships, flagships, and carrier escorts.

The Aircraft Carrier.

The aircraft carrier is the center of the fleet, able to strike at massive distances with her aircraft, consisting of fighters, dive bombers, torpedo bombers, multi-purpose fighters, ground attack aircraft, reconnaissance aircraft, and helicopters. The carrier truly is the epitome of naval power.

The Submarine.

The submarine is a powerful killer that operates mainly beneath the waves. The sub carries various anti-ship and anti-surface weapons such as torpedoes, ballistic missiles, nuclear missiles, and tomahawk missiles.
